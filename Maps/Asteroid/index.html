Asteroid¶

Asteroids are randomly located chunks of rock scattered floating around the station. There are 5 asteroids at round start. Each asteroid has a set layout, but randomly generated ore and somewhat randomly generated threats; mostly lots of Xenomorphs.

These rocks are where miners go to die.
